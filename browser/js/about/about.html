About Me

I'm a software engineer who is passionate about Education.

After graduating Wesleyan University, I spent three years teaching middle school science in East Harlem. I taught myself programming in order to use it with my students to analyze data. I quickly discovered my passion for it, and decided to attend Fullstack Academy, a 13 week intensive software development bootcamp.

Following that, I decided to attend the Recurse Center, a self-directed, educational retreat for programmers in order to improve my skills, learn new technologies, and study more theoretical concepts. While attending, I worked on hardware simmulation and web scraping, built a game engine, learned React and Redux, and collaborated on various projects with other software engineers.

I have extensive experience in the MEAN stack, as well as familiarity with MySQL, PostgreSQL, React, C++, and Python. Most importantly, I learn new technologies quickly.
