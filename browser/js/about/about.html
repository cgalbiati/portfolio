About Me

I'm a software engineer who is passionate about Education.

After graduating Wesleyan University, I spent three years teaching middle school science in East Harlem. While teaching, I taught myself to program in order to use it with my students to analyze data. I quickly discovered my passion for it, and decided to attend Fullstack Academy, a 13 week intensive software development bootcamp.

Following that, I decided to attend the Recurse Center, a self-directed, educational retreat for programmers in order to improve my skills, learn new technologies, and study more theoretical concepts. While attending, I worked on hardware simmulation, built a game engine, learned React, Redux and web scraping, and collaborated on various other projects with other software engineers.

I have extensive experience in the MEAN stack and PostgreSQL, as well as familiarity with MySQL, React, C++, and Python. Moreover, I learn new technologies quickly.
